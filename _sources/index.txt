=========
|project|
=========

A fast launcher for keyboard ninjas on Windows.

.. image:: images/wndrun-search2.png
    :align: center

* **Download** the latest release: |latest_link| |br|
  Requires Windows **64-bit** (7, 8, 8.1 or 10) |br|
  **Freeware**, no nag screen, no time limit, no ads
* Take a look at the :doc:`install` procedure or the :doc:`update`
* Read the :doc:`first` chapter
* Check out the :doc:`changelog` to know what's new

Need Help?
  * Join the |chat_room|
  * :ref:`contribute-issue`

You like |project|?
  * :ref:`contribute-packages`
  * Give it a **Like** or a **Star** to show your support: |br|
    |social_buttons|
